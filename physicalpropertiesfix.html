Physical Properties Of Methanol





Methanol is a colorless fairly volatile liquid with a faintly sweet pungent odor like that of ethyl alcohol. Completely mixes with water. Flammable and toxic. Physical Properties of methanol are:
Physical Properties Values
Boiling Point 65 ℃
Melting Point -98 ℃
Molecular weight 32.042 kg/kmol
Solubility 100 mg/mL at 70 ℉
Density 0.792 at 68 ℉
pH 7,2
Viscosity 0.544 mPa sec at 25 ℃
